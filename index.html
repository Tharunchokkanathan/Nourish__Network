Our Mission

Bridging the gap between excess and access.

Reducing food waste while nourishing communities.

Every day, tons of perfectly good food from restaurants, hotels, and events go to waste while millions face hunger. Nourish Network was built to solve this dual problem through an efficient, transparent tech platform.

We empower businesses to easily donate their surplus food, and we ensure it reaches vetted NGOs, orphanages, and old-age homes safely and quickly.

  • Verified network of recipients and donors.
  • Real-time tracking and logistics support.
  • Strict quality and safety guidelines.

How It Works

A seamless process to ensure food goes to plates, not landfills.

01

Upload Surplus

Restaurants or hotels list high-quality surplus food on our platform with details on quantity and packaging.

02

Quality Verification

Our rapid system checks food safety parameters to ensure only fit-for-consumption meals proceed.

03

NGOs Request

Vetted nearby NGOs and shelters receive alerts and can claim the food they need instantly.

Swift Delivery

Food is collected and delivered safely before expiry, ensuring a warm meal for those in need.
